About Me

Hey there! I'm Cheryl Harsch, a dedicated Registered Massage Therapist (RMT) passionate about patient-centered care. What really matters to me as a person and as a massage practitioner is the value I can provide.

EveryBODY that I have an opportunity to meet is treated in a respectful, non-judgmental, honest, and confidential manner. I support everyBODY through the experience and encourage open communication to ensure that I am meeting YOUR objectives and expectations.

Beyond Your Surface Massage, located in Edmonton AB, is open with flexible hours evenings and weekends. DIRECT BILLING offered.

Cheryl Harsch, RMT, is dedicated to patient-centered care by supporting and inspiring self-empowerment to be an active advocate for your own health and well-being, through massage and manual therapies, connecting your body awareness and educating prevention. She believes this is a fundamental part of aiding you to achieve lasting results, maintain and improve your overall quality of life.

Cheryl offers a variety of modalities and techniques; and treatments are available for youth, teens, adults (including pre-natal), and seniors. Refer to online booking system Noterro to view availability, your investment, and treatment durations.
